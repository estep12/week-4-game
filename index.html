CrystalsCollector!

A random number will be generated and given to you at the start of the game.


There are four crystals displayed. By clicking on a crystal, a specific amount will be added to your total score. The value of the crystals are hidden from you until you click on it.


To win the game, you must match your total score to the random number given to you. If your total score goes above the random number, you lose.


Each time you start the game, a new number will be generated and the values of the crystals will change.


Good Luck!!
